Top Travel Destinations in Egypt: Cairo, Luxor, Aswan & Alexandria

15 Jun 2026 Ahmed Farghally

Egypt offers a different mix of ancient history, Natural Views, and cultural experiences that captivates every traveler and make them admire Egypt and have the desire to travel to it again. From the pyramids of Giza and famous temples and tombs to many local markets, Egypt also provides a cultural trips. you can also visit many different destinations such as Cairo, Luxor, Aswan and Alexandria. You can include a Nile cruise in your trip. The beauty of Egypt really shines through the beauty of the Nile River, stunning red sea coast and the golden desert. It will an enjoyable experience, that you will never regret it. Book now with Sola dahabiya which is one of the best dahabiya nile cruises to explore the country and enjoy every single minute in your vacation

Trips to Egypt are designed to be suitable for every traveler, If you want to explore the history or seeking adventure. With your family or your partner. Sola dahabiya can provide everything for you. Families can have an enjoyable trip and visit the famous museums, also make a visit to the pyramids and cruising the nile, while couples can enjoy romantic moments watching the stunning sunset in luxor or relaxing on the red sea coast. The history lovers will enjoy a lot the trips which include visits to temples, tombs and monuments that bring ancient civilization to life, while the adventure seekers enjoy the desert safaris, nile sailing or diving in crystal clear waters

Our Travel packages are carefully customized to be fun and hassle free. You can choose an 8 days cairo and Nile Cruise by plane, where you will find the beauty of the city mixes with the sailing along the Nile. For those who want to mix cultural heritage with coastal beauty, The 10 days tours to Cairo, Alexandria, and Sharm El Sheikh will provide best balance. If you prefer long trips, you can book 13 days 12 nights Cairo, Nile Cruise, and Lake Nasser by flight will let you discover both the famous landmarks and the hidden gems. There is also another option is the 11 days 10 nights to Cairo, Nile Cruise, and Hurghada, which you can do both exploring the history and relaxing on beaches. Also we don’t forget the travelers who have short vacation. They can book 7 days 6 nights to Cairo and Aswan including Abu simbel.
